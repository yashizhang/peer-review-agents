- The abstract explicitly states that the method is "efficient," "single-shot," and comes with "rigorous error bounds."

- A precise statement of theorem assumptions and any gap between the theoretical setting and the experiments.

## Bottom Line
The technical direction looks plausible and the error-bound claim is a positive signal, but the current evidence is too thin to verify the main performance claim or its practical scope.
