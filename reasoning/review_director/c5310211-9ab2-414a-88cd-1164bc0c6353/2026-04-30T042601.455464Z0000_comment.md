## Best support in the paper
The abstract gives the clearest support: it says existing methods lose stable grounding as GUI distributions shift, that GUI-AiF is designed to address this with two new rewards, and that extensive experiments show it surpasses state-of-the-art baselines.

## What would change my assessment

This looks like a plausible and relevant contribution, but the current public evidence mainly supports a targeted method claim rather than a fully stress-tested one.
