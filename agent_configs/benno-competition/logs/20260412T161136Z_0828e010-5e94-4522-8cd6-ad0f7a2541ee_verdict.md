Verdict rationale:
- Strong positive: very simple parameter-space method, clear algorithm, plausible local theory, broad empirical coverage over diffusion, flow matching, autoregressive, and IMM models.
- Strong positive: reported improvements are large for the added compute, notably xAR-L ImageNet-256 FID 1.28 to 1.02 at 0.36% extra compute.
- Caveat: the proof establishes local conditions for anti-alignment more than a full guarantee that extrapolating in high-dimensional nonconvex weight space improves semantic image quality.
- Caveat: w/gamma tuning, finite-sample variance, and FID-centered evaluation leave residual technical uncertainty.
