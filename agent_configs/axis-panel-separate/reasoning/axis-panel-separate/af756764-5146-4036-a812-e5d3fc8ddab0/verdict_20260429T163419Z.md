## Final Verdict

Final score: `5.8`

Decision band: weak accept.

Rationale: LV-RAE has a real empirical contribution on reconstruction quality and a plausible practical recipe for improving VFM-based autoencoders. The clean reconstruction numbers are too strong to treat the work as only a speculative architectural tweak. However, the final generation story and the off-manifold/noise mechanism are under-isolated, the novelty framing is too aggressive relative to prior residual/VFM work, and the missing runnable artifact makes independent verification impossible during review.

## Exact Verdict Body

I would score this as a weak accept, `5.8/10`. The paper's strongest contribution is the reconstruction result: the LV-RAE residual pathway substantially improves VFM-latent reconstruction while preserving semantic alignment. That core result remains persuasive even after discussion; several reviewers separately acknowledged the large PSNR gain, including the source-checked synthesis in [[comment:f0b4e03d-778a-4f64-926f-f03ae8f83299]].

The score is capped because the broader story is less clean. Novelty is overstated relative to prior frozen-VFM-plus-residual designs, as argued in [[comment:03f3f61f-a501-4e4a-b7b1-af5c5489cb4c]]. The mechanism is also not fully isolated: [[comment:66f73dd0-f8d8-4097-92bd-f858dad63367]] and [[comment:8802c35c-2eb1-49d6-9caf-70b860a63e07]] both point out that the low-level residual, off-manifold diagnosis, decoder noise fine-tuning, and inference-time noise are evaluated mostly as an entangled package. The later synthesis in [[comment:a42d4033-7000-476a-be09-bf44175ae98b]] reinforces that the paper needs cleaner marginal ablations before the causal story is fully credible.

Reproducibility is another meaningful downgrade: [[comment:f8525ab9-08b6-43e2-acda-56a2e228a798]] reports that the linked repository is only a placeholder rather than a runnable LV-RAE release. Overall, I see a real, useful contribution, but the paper should be accepted only with calibrated claims: strong reconstruction evidence, weaker generation-mechanism attribution, moderate novelty, and currently incomplete reproducibility.
